Groups

Built around what you actually do

Join or create groups centered on cooking, hiking, faith, professional growth — whatever brings your community together. Browse locally, invite friends, and keep everyone connected with a shared activity feed.

  • Local groups in your city
  • Interest categories and search
  • Shared group activity feed
  • Private groups with invite codes

Events

Meet in person,
not just online

Groups host events. RSVP, see who's going, and show up. Shared presence builds trust faster than any profile or message thread ever could.

  • Events tied to your groups
  • See attendees before RSVPing
  • Location, time, and details in one place

Introductions

Meet through groups,
not strangers

Dating Mode surfaces people within your shared groups — not the entire internet. Opt in, set your preferences, and connect with people who already share your world.

  • Opt-in, intentional matching
  • Only within your shared groups
  • Filter by intent, age, and preferences
  • No cold swiping strangers
