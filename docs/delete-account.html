Account Deletion

How to request deletion

In the Godena app, go to Profile, open the settings list, and select Delete Account. Confirm the deletion request in the app. Your account will be deactivated immediately.

What data is deleted

Your profile, groups, events, connections, and associated account data are scheduled for permanent deletion after the restore window closes.

Retention period

After you request deletion, your account enters a 30-day deactivation window. During this time the account is disabled and not active for normal use.

What can be kept temporarily

Account data may be retained during the 30-day restore window so the account can be recovered if you change your mind within that period.

How to request a restore

If you want to restore your account within 30 days of the deletion request, email support@godena.app. After the 30-day window, deletion is permanent.

Contact

For account deletion or restore questions, contact support@godena.app.
