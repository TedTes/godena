Policy Sections

What We Collect

We collect profile details, group activity signals, chat content, and event participation data needed to run introductions and conversations.

How We Use Your Data

Your data powers matching, group participation, safety review, and product reliability. We do not expose private data publicly or sell it to third parties.

Who Can See What

Access is controlled by authentication and row-level security policies. Users can only access data they are explicitly permitted to view.

Safety and Moderation

Reports and blocks are private. We review flagged content for policy violations and may restrict accounts to protect community safety.

Account Deletion

Deleting your account starts a deactivation window. During this period your account is disabled and can be restored before final deletion.

Contact

For data, privacy, or safety questions, contact support@godena.app.
